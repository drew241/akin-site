Passive House

The Passive House standard is a highly energy efficient building standard that reduces a building's ecological footprint while achieving a comfortable temperature year-round with minimal energy use, making conventional heating and air conditioning systems obsolete.

Prefab modular

The building is constructed off-site, under controlled conditions, using the same materials and design as conventional on-site building — but in a fraction of the time. Buildings are produced in panels that, when assembled on site, reflect the identical design intent and specifications of Passive House standards.

Building with intention

Our designs support long term climate sustainability / net zero, aging in place, and your community vernacular. Passive House is not only one of the world's leading energy efficiency standards but also a construction concept made to build comfortable, environmentally friendly and affordable homes and buildings.
